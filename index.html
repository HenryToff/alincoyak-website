Export Services

Our team understands how critical flexible and custom-designed solutions are for Export Services. Supplies that are often moved to remote sites require accurate handling and on-time delivery. Regardless of your business needs, Alinco-Yak is here to tackle any problem you are facing. Our partnership with Export Service across the World and our extensive network of carriers allows us to provide any type of shipping that your business needs. Shipping of specific materials, equipment, and over-dimensional items require different transportation modes and customized vehicles. This means it is critical to work with carriers with strong industry expertise to meet carrier management standards for safety, insurance, and delivery service reliability. Finding a reliable logistics partner like Alinco-Yak Venture can make the transportation process easier and more cost-efficient for your company.
